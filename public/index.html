I am a 6'2" Setter/Right Side/Middle and captain at Lakeville North High School, as well as a member of the Mizuno Northern Lights Juniors Club Team. I have verbally committed to play Division I volleyball at Creighton University.

2023-2024 Season Highlights

  • Led high school varsity team in kills, aces, and assists
  • Star Tribune All-Metro First Team
  • Minnesota All-State honors
  • Pioneer Press East Metro Player of the Year finalist
  • USA Nationals Championship - 17 American Division
  • Named MVP and All-Tournament Team

This past club season, I helped lead my team to a USA Nationals championship in the 17 American division, where I was named MVP and selected to the All-Tournament Team. I was also selected to attend the National Team Development Program for USA Volleyball at the Olympic Training Center.

Recent Achievements (2025)

  • Reached 2,000 career assists, 1,000 kills, and 500 digs milestones
  • Star Tribune Athlete of the Week
  • MNVBCA All-State Tournament Team
  • JVA All-Nationals Team Selection
  • Ranked #1 Setter in Minnesota for Class of 2026

I pride myself on being a versatile athlete who excels in multiple positions. My coach describes me as having "size, skills, and finesse to be a high level setter at the next level" with "consistent location" and the ability to "run a number of different tempos on offense."
