COMMITTED

I am a 6’2” Setter/Right Side/Middle and captain at Lakeville North High School, as well as a member of the Mizuno Northern Lights Juniors Club Team. I have verbally committed to play Division I volleyball at Creighton University. In 2023, I led my high school varsity team in kills, aces, and assists, earned Star Tribune All-Metro First Team and Minnesota All-State honors, and was a finalist for the Pioneer Press East Metro Player of the Year. This past club season, I helped lead my team to a USA Nationals championship in the 17 American division, where I was named MVP and selected to the All-Tournament Team.

Rayna Christianson never expected to experience a coaching change going into her senior volleyball season at Lakeville North.
